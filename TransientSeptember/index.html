The Transient September

There was a book I read as a kid called The Wump World. The story featured peaceful wombat-like creatures that lived undisturbed on a lush green planet. Along come some heavily industrialized, overly numerous aliens that ravage the surface. The atmosphere and environment quickly becomes a toxic ruin and the aliens all leave for a yet another target of their desecration. The wumps emerge from their hiding as new life germinates in the cracks of concrete.

The normies are leaving the internet. They are moving on to their places. Tik-tok, Instagram, Amazon; the non-internet.

They leave behind a devastated internet. A ravaged landscape of ads, bots, and spam.

Let them go! Cyberspace was never their space and they were never welcome among us.

In their absence, Something new is being reborn
